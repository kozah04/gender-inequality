# Gender Inequality in Education and Employment

Statistical analysis of gender gaps in educational attainment, labour force participation, and wage outcomes across African economies and selected global comparators.

## Research Questions

- RQ1: Is there a statistically significant gender gap in educational attainment across African countries and selected comparators, and has it narrowed over time?
- RQ2: Does the gender gap in labour force participation differ significantly across African regions and income groups, and what does the trend look like over the last three decades?
- RQ3: Does gender predict wage outcomes when controlling for education level and employment sector, and does the gender wage gap vary by education level?
- RQ4: Does progress in closing the education gender gap predict a corresponding reduction in the labour force participation gap across countries?

## Data Sources

- World Bank WDI via wbgapi - education and labour force participation indicators
- ILOSTAT bulk download - disaggregated wage data by sex and sector

## Countries

Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa, Ethiopia, Brazil, India, Germany, Bangladesh

## Period

1990 to 2023

## Setup

```bash
conda env create -f environment.yml
conda activate gender-inequality
```

Run notebooks in order:

1. `notebooks/eda.ipynb` - pulls data on first run, caches to `data/raw/`, runs coverage audit and EDA
2. `notebooks/modelling.ipynb` - loads from cache, runs all statistical tests and models

## Outputs

- `outputs/reports/` - tracked. Test results, regression coefficients.
- `outputs/figures/` - gitignored. All charts generated by notebooks.
- `data/` - gitignored. Raw and processed parquet files.
